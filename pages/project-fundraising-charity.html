Fundraising automation

Multi-million pound charitable enterprise

Date Nov 2025 Build time 2 hours Saves 6.5 days/year

Grant applications took a full day each because staff were repeatedly finding funders and re-entering the same charity information into similar forms.

Quickler built a system that stored the charity’s core information and created draft forms automatically. Staff could enter project needs, get matching funders, and start from pre-filled applications instead of blank pages.

Eight hours dropped to roughly ten minutes. Across six forms per year, that translated into 6.5 working days returned to higher-value charitable work.
